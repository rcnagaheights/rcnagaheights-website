President's Message

A Welcome From Our Club President

There are moments that define a club. Not because of the meetings we hold or the projects we launch, but because of the courage to dream bigger than ourselves. This is one of those moments. Welcome to the Rotary Club of Naga Heights.

As I begin my year of service as President, I do so with profound gratitude and an even greater sense of responsibility. Leadership in Rotary is never about holding a position. It is about carrying a promise — a promise that every decision we make, every partnership we build, and every life we touch will leave our communities better than we found them. That is why our call this Rotary Year is simple yet demanding: Create Lasting Impact.

This conviction is embodied in our presidential thrust, BAKAS — our commitment to champion Buhay at Kalusugan, Aral at Karunungan, Katarungan at Kapayapaan, Angat na Kabuhayan, and Serbisyo at Kapaligirang Pangmatagalan. More than five pillars, they are the legacy we aspire to leave behind.

This website is more than a window into our club. It is an open invitation. Whether you are a fellow Rotarian, a community partner, a civic leader, or someone who simply believes that the world can become better through service, I invite you to journey with us. Together, let us leave a BAKAS that truly creates lasting impact.

Atty. Khristian Damielle Jamer, RN, LPT, JD

Club President, Rotary Year 2026–2027
