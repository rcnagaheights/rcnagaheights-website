What is DTC

The DiskwenTulong Card

DiskwenTulong Card (DTC) is more than a discount card, it is a card with a purpose.

Every use unlocks exclusive discounts and privileges from partner local businesses while helping fund life-changing community service projects of the Rotary Club of Naga Heights.

By choosing the DTC, you don't just save money, you empower local enterprises, uplift communities, and become part of a movement that transforms everyday purchases into lasting impact. Save more. Help more. CREATE LASTING IMPACT.
