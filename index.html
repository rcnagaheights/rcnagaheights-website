Who We Are

People of Action, Rooted in Naga

Rotary connects leaders and problem-solvers who act to create lasting change. The Rotary Club of Naga Heights applies these ideals through education, health, the environment, livelihoods, and fellowship.

Rotary

THE FOUR-WAY TEST

Of the things we think, say or do:

first

Is it the TRUTH?

second

Is it FAIR to all concerned?

third

Will it build GOODWILL and BETTER FRIENDSHIPS?

fourth

Will it be BENEFICIAL to all concerned?
