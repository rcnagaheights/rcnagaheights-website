Presidential Message

There are moments that define a club. Not because of the meetings we hold or the projects we launch, but because of the courage to dream bigger than ourselves.

This is one of those moments.

Welcome to the Rotary Club of Naga Heights.

As I begin my year of service as President, I do so with profound gratitude and an even greater sense of responsibility. Leadership in Rotary is never about holding a position. It is about carrying a promise. A promise that every decision we make, every partnership we build, and every life we touch will leave our communities better than we found them.

That is why our call this Rotary Year is simple yet demanding: Create Lasting Impact.

Impact is not measured by applause. It is measured by the child whose future is forever changed because someone believed in them. By the family given hope when hope seemed impossible. By the community empowered to rise long after the project has ended. Real service is not remembered because it was seen. It is remembered because it changed lives.

This conviction is embodied in our presidential thrust, BAKAS.

BAKAS is our commitment to champion Buhay at Kalusugan, Aral at Karunungan, Katarungan at Kapayapaan, Angat na Kabuhayan, and Serbisyo at Kapaligirang Pangmatagalan. More than five pillars, they are the legacy we aspire to leave behind. A legacy of healthier lives, empowered minds, stronger communities, sustainable livelihoods, and a future where service continues to flourish across generations.

As one of the youngest clubs in Rotary District 3820, we refuse to believe that age defines influence. We believe that vision does. Passion does. Action does. Every challenge before us is an invitation to innovate. Every partnership is an opportunity to multiply our impact. Every member is a leader capable of transforming lives.

This website is more than a window into our club. It is an open invitation.

Whether you are a fellow Rotarian, a community partner, a civic leader, or someone who simply believes that the world can become better through service, I invite you to journey with us. Together, we can build projects that endure, raise leaders who inspire, and create a legacy that future generations will proudly inherit.

The greatest footprints are not the ones we leave on the ground.

They are the ones we leave in the hearts of people.

Together, let us leave a BAKAS that truly creates lasting impact.

ATTY. KHRISTIAN DAMIELLE JAMER, RN, LPT, JD
President, Rotary Year 2026–2027
Rotary Club of Naga Heights
